TL-DR

This Chrome extension provides users with highlights of the terms and conditions of websites that are known to collect data. They will provide this information when people are considering signing up for a service and when they are using the service. 

features

sleek and simple interface

Clicking on the extension opens up a panel that is both easy to read and navigate. This panel has three options to toggle between: Terms Made Easy, Read the Full Terms, and Resources.

  • Terms Made Easy
  • Read the Full Terms
  • Resources

Terms Made Easy

  • Viewer-friendly bullet pointed list of information
  • Outlines important terms and conditions of viewed site
  • Encourages users to stay informed

Read the Full Terms

  • View the full terms of a platform in a popout page
  • Allows for comparison between full and simplified terms and conditions

Resources

  • Links to additional information, includes both readings and videos
  • Consider: Why Read Terms and Conditions?
  • Consider: How to Scan Terms and Conditions?
